# Brix — Signal Tracker for Bybit

Real-time breakout signal tracker for Bybit Futures & Spot. Free Android app.

---

## What is Brix?

Brix monitors Bybit markets and notifies you the moment a breakout signal is detected — even when your screen is off. Open the app to see a live feed of BUY and SELL signals with real-time price tracking and P&L for each position.

## How to Use

**1. Install & open**
Install the APK and open Brix. Watch a short video ad to unlock the signal feed.

**2. Browse signals**
Signals appear as cards showing the coin, direction (BUY or SELL), signal price, and live current price with P&L.

**3. Switch markets**
Tap **Futures** or **Spot** at the top to switch between market types.

**4. Sort signals**
Tap the filter icon to sort by **Latest**, **Top Gainers**, or **Top Losers**.

**5. View chart**
Tap **View Chart** on any card to open the full TradingView chart for that signal.

**6. Notifications**
Brix runs in the background and sends a push notification whenever a new signal is detected.

**7. Pull to refresh**
Pull down anywhere on the feed to trigger an immediate scan.
